Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorites fodds.here is recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in afrying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the egs and cream in a bawl
  3. Once butter has metled add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the egde of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
